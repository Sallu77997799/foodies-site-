Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes, we offer free delivery on all orders above Rs. 1500 within a 10km radius.

Absolutely. 100% of our meat and ingredients are certified Halal.

Yes! You can add extra cheese, extra patties, or remove certain toppings by mentioning it in your order notes.
